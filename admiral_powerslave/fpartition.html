Format F: For Stock HDD




As you may, or may not know, the F: drive partition is not accessable in the FTP client with a Stock HDD.  The Xbox HDD is 10GB, but Microsoft only chose to use 8Gb of that space.  So, if you want an extra 2GB from your stock HDD, then add this to your "evox.ini" file.''  This does not work with the Western Digital drives, as they ARE only 8GB.

Remember, backup your HDD if you are not comfortable with these brain storms.

Item "Install F drive",@215

Then add this section to the very bottom of the ini:
[Action_15]

Info "Install F_Drive HDD"
Warning "This will not format all drives, Only the F Partition."
Warning "Make sure you have a backup of C & E anyway."
Progress "Formatting drive"
ConfigSector "\backup\disk.bin"
Format f:

After you save the changes to the ini file, reboot and choose Install F drive from the menu.

As you know, F drive is not EMPTY or NULL anyway, this will get you 2GB of space for some applications, except with WD 8GB drives.  Better than nothing, huh?
