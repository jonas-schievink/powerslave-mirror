Believe it or not, you can flash your Xboxs TSOP with an Evox bios, rather than put in a mod chip.

V1.0 Xbox's have a 1MB chip, that can be bank-switched, so you can have both a standard, and NON standard BIOS.

You still have to open it up, and solder wires to turn off the write protect on the TSOP.

http://www.eurasia.nu/wiki/index.php?pagename=XboxFlashWithoutModchipJaa

You'll find the information you need there.  Remember, no one is responsible for damages you do!

V1.7 Xbox's DO NOT have a flashable TSOP - They switched to ROM/PROM only.
