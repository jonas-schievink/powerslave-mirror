 INSIGNIA

From left to right:  Logo used during Kirk's second command of the Enteprise-A, Logo worn as belt buckle and/or on unform during Kirks second command of Entperise, Logo used during Captain Picard's first Enteprise D 7 year mision, Latest lago used by Starfleet, Logo worn on bottom left of uniform top (two piece suit).
 

  The following insignia have been recreated by Enterprise crew, who, though time travel have seen, or have had visions of them. These designs have never been used by Starfleet, they are simply the visions of others.

Starfleet, after learning of their designs,  has considered using these altered designs under certain circumstances. The Bottom would optimally support SubSpace radio signals. The top left insignia to be used as a special forces insignia, or Starfleet marine vessels..

Note: Rank is indicated on the this  insignia, by the number of gold bars, following the same suit as the circular "pips", except the rank of Admiral, and Junior Grades, are not supported in this design. One could interpret a Junior Grade with only the left half of the bar gold, while the right other half remains silver. The design also shows, that, all four bars are always present, only gold filled represent rank.

Starfleet changes it's rank and insignia almost every two, to five years. There is no danger of running out of ideas here!
