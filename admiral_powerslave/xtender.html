X-TENDER CASE MOD

This is a complete kit that will allow you convert your Xbox™ into a mini rack mount server or media center with activity LED's and expanded space for 2 internal Hard Drives.


Molded to perfectly integrate with your Xbox™, this kit comes with a custom front panel, with 3 activity LED, a custom made hard drive mounting plate to accommodate one or two additional drives, and a custom molded rear panel. Specially designed


 X-Tender rods are included to internally support the expansion, allowing you to security reassemble the machine. Additional space on front panel will allow the support of one LCD Display on each side of the system, or additional LEDs or Cold Cathode Lights. You can switch between the newly added drives via the buttons at the front.

The kit comes with all parts you need, including an exhaustive installation guide.
