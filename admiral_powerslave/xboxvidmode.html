PAL TO NTSC
Using Xbox Media Center (XBMC)

It depends on the sections you have activated/hidden in the config.xml in your main menu. For example if you use the TV-Guide you have to move down 3 times.

If you have accidentally changed your video to PAL or your video mode got changed to PAL somehow, here is how to fix it with XBMC.

1. Start xbmp (from manu, or boot DVD/cdrw for example)
2. press down 2 times (using dpad or remote control) to move to the settings menu.  3 times if you have the TV-Guide option active.
3. press A to get into the settings menu
4. press down 5 times to get select the video menu
5. press A to get into the video menu
6. press right to select the ' tv standard' control
7. press right to select the up control
8. press A this will select the NTSC-M standard
9. press down 3 times to select the SET button
10. press A to hit the button

Your box should be NTSC again

The best way is to burn a "Enigmah Video Mode Switch Disc", boot from it and press "B" (NTSC) or "A" (Pal) and reboot.

I could not find a way within the M$Dash to set the video mode.  I went to settings, and video mode, but all there is, is the screen settings, and nothing else.

You can also buy an Adapter to go between the XBox and TV.

There is almost NO information on changing video modes, and nothing at all for doing it with the M$dash.  All I could find was using the Enigma video switcher.

You can put that in your APPS directory on Xbox -via- FTP.  If you can select it from the APPS menu, then you simply need to execute it, and press "B" for NTSC or "A" for PAL and reboot.

Changing video modes can now get you banned on Xbox live.
