XBOX LIVE ALTERNATIVES

XBConnect; is "Next Generation" Game Console Tunnel Software for Windows that allows you to play Microsoft XBox System link, and Sony PSP games over the internet.

XBConnect acts like a bridge between your game console and the internet. Its like plugging your console into a lan that stretches around the world. Your friends can be on the other side of the planet and you can still link up for gaming fun.

Your PC must be on, and signed in to XBC in order for this to work.  Basically, it makes the WAN look like the LAN for link play.  The Xbox operates through the PC, then to XBC.  I have used this briefly, but still doesn't compare to the Online Experiance XBL offers, AND, there is no downloadable content.


XLink Kai; is a global gaming network - bringing together XBox, Playstation 2, Gamecube and PSP users, in one integrated community. It is software running on your PC or Macintosh that allows you to play system-link enabled games online for free. Kai is the only service that is not console specific, and boasts one of the friendliest communites available.

This works like XBC software does, on the PC, and tunneled through it, and again, no downloadable content.
