What you need to know (Basic knowledge):
Standard BIOS (Basic Input Output System) is the program in the chip that starts up your Xbox and communicates between the devices in the xbox such as the hard drive and graphics card. The BIOS is extremely important to your Xbox system. An xbox mod chip replaces the default Microsoft BIOS with its own "hacked" BIOS. The hacked BIOS will allow you to run unauthorized programs.  The Xbox is, basically, a non-legacy PC, you can look that up.  The XBOX bios requires the HDD to be locked, with a stored password, before execution of any code; a security measure.  This locking prevents access to the drive when removed, powered off, or intialized.   The standard bios also checks for an electronic signature (protection) in the program(s) before executing any code from the HDD or DVD drive.

Cromwell Bios: Most mod chips today are shipped with some version of the Cromwell Bios, which is a totally legal bios that was not designed with any infringement of, or copyrighted Microsoft code. When you boot your Xbox, with a mod chip enabledthat has a Cromwell Bios installed, you are overriding the Microsoft Xboxs Bios.  The Cromwell bios is often used as a starting point for installing another bios.  Many Xbox modders mod their xbox solely for the purpose of installing Linux. In this case, Cromwell is perfect because with it you can get complete control over the xbox boot process, allowing you to turn your Xbox into a Linux-only machine. With the Cromwell bios you can repartition and format the hard disk completely for Linux, and erase all traces of the original Xbox bios.

Non Standard Bios:  These bioses, known as EvoX_M series, Executor, and so on, allow the xbox to boot past all  Microsoft's security checks, both on the HDD and DVD drives.  They allow the execution of what is called non-signed code, which is another security measure to be sure it is a Microsoft Liscensed product.  The bios also allows booting of backups on DVD and some CDRs (if apllication/game small enough) media.  Booting of a backup with the original bios will fail 100% of the time, there is no way around it.  Altering the contents of the HDD without a mod chip will fail, unless the O/S is explioted (soft modded)..

Dashboard: An Xbox "dashboard" is what you see when your Xbox boots up without a disc in the dvd-rom drive. It's the GUI (graphical user interface) you see. When you install an alternate dashboard, like EvolutionX, then you are installing an alternate interface, with lots of additional features. The menu setup on most of alternate dashboards can be configured so that it features, tools, and utilities that you can add to your modded Xbox to be run. In some ways, you could think of the dashboard as your Xbox's operating system, like Windows is on your home PC.  Most alternate dashboards come with built-in FTP servers. An FTP server is necessary so that you can connect your computer to your Xbox -via- networking to transfer files bank and forth.
