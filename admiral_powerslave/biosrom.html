BIOS ROM INFORMATION

This is limited information, because M$ says there is sensitive information in the code and is not allowed to be posted publicly.  However, for those of you who want to extract the FLASH ROM contents of the Xbox for your own purposes, see the case of  "Sony Computer Entertainment, Inc. v. Connectix Corp." You are actually allowed by law to reverse engineer copyrighted code so long as it is necessary to discover the ideas or functional elements behind the code So,  Microsoft, what are the ideas and functional elements behind your BIOS ROM? It would also seem that a patent search turns up nothing on Xbox.. I guess you'll just have to reverse engineer it. (FTR, Nintendo has patented what looks to be the entirety of the N64 console, thus perchance making reverse engineering an N64 illegal--not yet court tested.)
