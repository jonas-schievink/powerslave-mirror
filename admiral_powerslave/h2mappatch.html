HALO II MAP PATCHING

1ST:
Get ppf-o-matic:  http://files.halomods.com/download.php?id=5
Get the necryptomatic signature tool:  http://www.tf6.epgservers.net/downloads/TF6_-_Halo2_ES.rar
Get Winrar: http://www.rarlab.com/download.htm
Have an FTP Client, of course.

NOTE:
Always make a backup of your original maps!
You need a modded xbox for this!  You should have Halo 2 on your HDD!

1) Now connect with your xbox using ftp, do this by adding your xbox ip-adress in the adres menu, your username (its in your xbox settings somewhere) in the username window, and your password (also in settings) in the pasword window.
* (Normally the username and pasword is just "xbox")

2) Once you connected to your xbox, look for your F:\ drive or any other drive where you put your Halo 2 game. Then go into the Halo 2 folder and look for a subfolder called maps.

3) Transfer the map you need from your xbox to your pc by clicking the map and move it to your pc (then ftp will auto copy the map.)

4) Open your donwloaded file, should be a .rar or .zip file (which means it is compressed), extract it to your pc.

5) Now you need to open ppf-o-matic. You should see two blank fields. In the upper you should put the original map (like you took it fro your xbox)
*To see these maps you have to change it from ISO files --> all files Exclamation In the lower field you should select your ppf.  Then hit apply.

6) Now that your map is patched with the mod. You need to "resign" it, otherwise you get a dirty disc error.  Now use the encryptomatic signature tool, open your map, then after succesfully signed you see a message which confirms the signing.

7) Transfer the map back to you xbox (overwrite the old one) from the PC modded one.

8 ) Play Halo 2. Sometimes you have to play a different map otherwise it wont load the modded map, this is easier than clearing the cache files!
