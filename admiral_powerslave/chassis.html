CASE MODS

Colored Shells:


Usually, the replacement is just the TOP shell, not the bottom part that the motherbaord is attached to.  There are many colors, including freen (for you Halo fans).

Other modifiactions include LEDs, Hard Disk activity LED, and other lighting, added USB ports, and "skins" to wrap it in.   You can find them all on Ebay.

There is only ONE true Halo Green chassis, and it is the entire case, not just the top shell.  These were from Microsoft, and will have the HALO logo on the right side of the unit.  People on Ebay try to sell by stating HALO GREEN chassis, but, I have the original Halo Edition Xbox, and they are not the same.  The official HALO edition is more a transluscent green, not 100% transparent like some of those ones on Ebay.
